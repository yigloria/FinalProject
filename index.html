Macaroon

The project named Everything About One Thing sumbmitted for ISTD 2014. These posters introduce the history and evolution of macaroon. It also includes a collection of how people perceive macaroon.

Meat Bar

“MEATBAR” is an identity design for an imaginary bar that produces the meatball. It is a graphic approach which uses the single color, simple lines and geometric shape to present different types of meatballs and flavours. These graphic elements and the background color are aim to targeting the younger audiences.

Everybody Has A Story

The magazine collects lots of different types of personal stories. Based on the magazine, the project “Visual Journalist” required us to choose a personal story or an interview, then, re-edit it by using editor’s own voice.
